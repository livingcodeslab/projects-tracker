* Integration

** Explore New Chip Form Factor

The GFET-S20 chips are nice, but we are having trouble finalising the
integration with them.

We are looking into using the newer =mGFET-4D=[fn:1] with the
=Opentrons OT-2=[fn:2] platform to automate as much of the sensing process as
possible.

We will need the =Graphenea card=[fn:3] to interface with the chips.

To make all the equipment mentioned above work together, we will need to make
some sort of adapter for the Graphene card to sit securely in one of the labware
bays provided by the OT-2 robot. In that respect, we look at the physical
specifications for both the labware[fn:4] and the graphenea card[fn:5].

The technical specifications indicate that it might be possible to create an
adapter of sorts to hold the Graphenea card snugly inside the OT-2 robot and
allow us to define some sort of custom labware that we would then use for the
automation.
