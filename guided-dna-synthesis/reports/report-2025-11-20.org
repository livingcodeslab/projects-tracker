* Integration

** Test Conductors

Testing for continuity with the multimeter, I found that there was no continuity
in one of the contacts of the cartridge. Further inspection showed that a solder
joint in the 8-wire circular female connector was broken.

I fixed the joint, and tested the continuity again. All lines have expected continuity.

I also checked the continuity and routing of the lines from the SMU connectors
all the way  to the 8-wire circular male connector. Everything works fine for that.
