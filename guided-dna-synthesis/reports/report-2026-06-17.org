** Opentrons OT-2 Robot

*** Mount and Calibrate Pipette

We begin the day by figuring out how to mount the =P20= pipette onto the robot.

Once mounted, it requires calibration - here, we ran into an issue with
non-responsiveness in the robot. I tried restarting the robot to see whether
that would help. It did.

I then proceeded to actually calibrate the robot.

I looked into live-streaming: the OT-2 cannot do that according to the
[[https://docs.opentrons.com/ot-2/opentrons-app/features-summary/#built-in-camera][device's documentation]].

*** Labware Offsets

We begin by trying to upload the dummy  protocol. The upload is successful, but
the protocol validation steps fail due to missing labware.

I attempt to import the custom labware, but the Opentrons App raises the error
=Invalid labware definition=. After looking around, I discovered the
[[https://github.com/Opentrons/opentrons/tree/edge/shared-data/labware/schemas][labware definition schemas]]. I use the version 2 schema on to validate the
definition and fix bugs.

These 2 commits fix the labware definition:

- https://github.com/livingcodeslab/projects-tracker/commit/17a0f7674ad54fc4bf75a19d3feb1ac38a765c90
- https://github.com/livingcodeslab/projects-tracker/commit/7b3abee11b89484e14cdca500995f207707550fe


Now that the labware definition is imported successfully, we can begin

with our definition. In that respect, the following commits include the results
