** Opentrons

*** Labware Definition

Custom labware definition files seem to need to be in the JSON file format[fn:1].

The cartridge does not sit snugly in the opentrons' slots for labware and tools.
Options:
- Design a new cartridge from scratch with opentrons in mind (long-term)
- Modify existing labware and cartridge to get usable mish-mash (short-term)
